# Custom gameplay assets

Generated with the built-in OpenAI ImageGen workflow on June 24, 2026.
Flat green or magenta chroma-key backgrounds were removed locally and the
results were cropped and optimized as transparent PNG files.

## Prompt set

- `spitter.png`: original overhead red ranged mutant, swollen crimson armor and
  glowing throat sac, mature hand-painted arcade style, compatible with Kenney
  top-down proportions.
- `spitter-orb.png`: molten red-orange bio-energy projectile with a bright core
  and short directional tail.
- `pickup-medkit.png`: rugged red-and-white futuristic medical case.
- `pickup-armor.png`: graphite ballistic plate with cyan energy edges.
- `pickup-weapon-crate.png`: black and hazard-yellow locked weapon supply case.
- `mutant-form.png`: original green player mutation form with containment bands
  and industrial survivor equipment; no third-party superhero costume, logo,
  face or branding.

All prompts required one isolated subject, direct overhead perspective, no
watermark, no scene background and no third-party branding.
